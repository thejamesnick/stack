**App Name:** Stack

**Full Description:**
Stack is a smart, easy-to-use mini-app designed to help you build consistent savings habits while keeping full control of your money. With Stack, you can choose an amount to save and select how often you want it to be pulled from your wallet—daily, weekly, or monthly. Your funds are automatically secured in a smart contract until your stacking goal is complete, so you can save without thinking about it.

**How It Works:**

1. **Set Your Stack:** Choose the amount, frequency (daily, weekly, or monthly), and duration for your savings goal.
2. **Automated Pulls:** Stack automatically transfers the chosen amount from your wallet into a secure smart contract at the schedule you set.
3. **Goal Completion:** At the end of your stacking period, you can withdraw your total savings.
4. **Flexible Early Exit:** If you need access to your funds before the plan ends, you can break your stack early. A small, transparent fee applies, helping Stack remain sustainable while encouraging discipline.
5. **Future Yield Integration:** Funds can optionally be routed into low-risk yield strategies, earning extra returns while you save, making your money work harder for you.

**Key Features:**

* **Daily, Weekly, Monthly Stacks:** Flexibly customize your savings plan to fit your lifestyle.
* **Automated Smart Contract Pulls:** No manual transfers—your plan runs automatically.

* **Future-Proof Yield Options:** Stack can grow your savings via optional DeFi or yield strategies.
* **Simple, Minimal Interface:** Focus on saving, not on unnecessary gamification or leaderboards.

**Revenue Model:**

* **Yield Spread:** When funds are routed into yield strategies, Stack keeps a small percentage of the earnings while passing the rest to users.
* **Premium Features (Future):** Custom stacking schedules, multiple simultaneous stacks, or higher limits for advanced users.
